About Me

Education

Hi, I'm Gabe Zeller, a student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign pursuing a degree in Geographic Information Science with a minor in Computer Science.

My passion lies in coding, and I have particularly enjoyed learning Java and C++ during my academic journey. This fall 2023 semester, I'm eagerly anticipating courses on Data Structures in C++ and Environmental Data Science, which will introduce me to coding in R.

While my precise career goals are yet to be defined, I find satisfaction in working with data through programming and plan to further explore this area. I'm on track to complete my degree in the Fall of 2025.

Triangle Fraternity

Outside of my academic pursuits, I actively participate in the Illinois chapter of Triangle Fraternity, a unique organization that brings together STEM majors. Initially, I had not considered joining Greek life, but during the second half of my freshman year, I discovered Triangle and was drawn to its distinctive community.

As a member, one of my most cherished experiences was serving as the float chair for the UIUC 2022 Homecoming parade. I took on the responsibility of designing and constructing our float, which featured a Stranger Things theme. I handcrafted a Demi-Dog from the show and, aiming for an ambitious touch, arranged for a live band of musically inclined Triangle members to play 80's music from the series on the moving float. Personal

I am a lover of live music, and I frequently attend local shows both in my hometown of Peoria and in the Champaign-Urbana area. I especially enjoy it when there is a mosh pit! In general, I enjoy most music, but my favorite is metal and hardcore. Currently, I love listening to Slipknot, Deftones, and Knocked Loose.

Fitness has been a significant part of my life since my high school cross-country days. While I may not always maintain a strict routine, I genuinely enjoy lifting at the gym and going for runs whenever possible. My interest in fitness extends to nutrition, and I pay close attention to my dietary choices to align them with my fitness goals.

In addition to my love for music and fitness, I also find joy in longboarding, especially around campus, exploring fashion through thrifting, spending time outdoors, appreciating the oxford comma, and learning about spirits and distilling — something I discovered after working at a local small batch distillery.
