About Me

Education

Hi, I'm Gabe Zeller and I am a student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign studying Geographic Information Science with a minor in Computer Science.

My favorite thing so far as a student has been learning to code in Java and C++ between various courses. I am looking forward to this fall 2023 semester where I will take Data Structures in C++ and a course titled Environmental Data Science where I will learn to code in R.

My exact goals are unknown, but so far I enjoy dealing with data via code and will keep exploring this avenue. I expect to complete my degree in the Fall of 2025.

Triangle Fraternity

Much of my time outside of academics is spent as a member of the Illinois chapter of Triangle Fraternity. I had no intention of joining greek life when I went to college, but during the second half of my freshman year, I learned about Triangle, a fraternity of STEM majors, and found this was a unique group I wanted to be a part of.

One of favorite memories was my experience as our float chair. This meant I was responsible for designing and building our float for the UIUC 2022 Homecoming parade. I went with a Stranger Things theme, hand-making a Demi-Dog from the show, and decided to be ambitious by having a live band on the float consisting of our more musical members who played 80's music from the show.
