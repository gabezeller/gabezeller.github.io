About Me

Education

Hi, I'm Gabe Zeller, a student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign pursuing a degree in Geographic Information Science with a minor in Computer Science.

My passion lies in problem solving. This is why I love programming in my coursework whether it's C++ for computer science or R for environmental data science. I am looking forward to this Spring semester where I will learn more GIS fundamentals and take a Game Development course, where I will learn to build video games using the Unreal Engine.

While my precise career goals are yet to be defined, I find satisfaction in working with data through programming and plan to further explore this area. I'm on track to complete my degree in the Fall of 2025.

Triangle Fraternity

Outside of my academic pursuits, I actively participate in the Illinois chapter of Triangle Fraternity, a unique organization that brings together STEM majors. Initially, I had not considered joining Greek life, but during the second half of my freshman year, I discovered Triangle and was drawn to its distinctive community.

I have held numerous positions in the house including Float chair, where I tapped into my creativity to design and build our float for the UIUC 2022 Homecoming parade, which featured a Stranger Things theme and a live band with music from the show. I have also served as Homecoming chair which put me in charge of planning and executing all homecoming weekend events for our alumni and active members to enjoy.

This coming Spring semester I will serve as Social Director, an executive board member responsible for planning and executing all social events for the chapter. I am excited to try some new innovative things the chapter hasn't done before and hopefully plan one of the most fun semesters my brothers have seen.

Personal

I am a lover of live music, and I frequently attend local shows both in my hometown of Peoria and in the Champaign-Urbana area. I especially enjoy it when there is a mosh pit! In general, I enjoy most music, but my favorite is metal and hardcore. Currently, I love listening to Slipknot, Deftones, Knocked Loose, and Lorna Shore.

Fitness has been a significant part of my life since my high school cross-country days. While I may not always maintain a strict routine, I genuinely enjoy lifting at the gym and going for runs whenever possible. My interest in fitness extends to nutrition, and I pay close attention to my dietary choices to align them with my fitness goals.

In addition to my love for music and fitness, I also find joy in longboarding, especially around campus, exploring fashion through thrifting, spending time outdoors, and learning about spirits and distilling — something I discovered after working at a local small batch distillery.
